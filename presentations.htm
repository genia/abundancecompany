Presentations by Bob Czimbal

Bob Czimbal's presentations are a delightful blend of: short lectures, inspiring stories, interactive exercises, skill building activities, lively group discussions, educational handouts, surprises and prizes. Presentations can be customized and blended to meet needs of the audience. The length of the presentation can vary from one hour to one day. The size of audience ranges from 20 to 2,000.

Audiences welcome the chance to learn new skills, laugh and make new friends. Bob specializes in using the wisdom and talents of the audience to enhance the presentation. Tools are provided to convert awareness to action. Attendees consistently report gaining skills that will last a lifetime.

Since 1970, Bob has delivered dynamic presentations to international conferences, corporations, small businesses, universities, hospitals and government agencies. Bob's professional career includes work as a Sociologist, Community Planner and founder of the Cherry Grove Retreat Center. Currently he is the director of the Abundance Company.
