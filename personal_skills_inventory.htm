Personal Skills Inventory

We have distilled a one-page  “Personal Skills Inventory”  through extensive research and stories of successful people. This quick inventory provides a systematic approach to evaluating strengths and areas for improvement. All of the skills and attributes are dynamically interwoven. The consequences of a weakness in even one of the skill sets can significantly reduce the efficacy of the others.  

Everyone has some degree of development in these areas. Use the instrument to discover where you are now and where you want to go. Invent your own system to evaluate your level of skill. One suggestion is to place a star by the each of the skill sets that you feel you have mastered. Then select one skill that you want to improve. Next, move on to creating a plan to develop this particular skill or attribute. Share your plan with your supervisor or coworkers and ask for support.  

“We have the answers. Do we have the courage to ask the right questions?”

Work teams can use the inventory as a feedback system. We will custom design a training program for you based on the skills that you select.
